The environment's parameters can be changed to generate different VEs. Parameters include: task or environment type, path lengths, path complexity (bends, turns, road texture) and indoor environ- ment (maze complexity). They didn't do any user studies or anything. It's simply an idea that they propose. It's nothing special, I think. **Bibliography**: [#sarupuri2018LUTE]: Sarupuri, Bhuvaneswari, Simon Hoermann, Mary C. Whitton, and Robert W. Lindeman.
